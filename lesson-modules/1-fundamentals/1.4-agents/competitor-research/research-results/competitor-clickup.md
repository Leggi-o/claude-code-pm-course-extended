# Competitor Analysis: ClickUp

## Features
- Tasks, sprints, Gantt charts, kanban boards
- Built-in docs, wikis, and whiteboards
- ClickUp Brain AI (task delegation, document creation)
- Time tracking built-in
- Chat functionality within platform
- Custom automations and workflows
- 1,000+ integrations

## Pricing
- **Free:** Unlimited tasks, 100MB storage
- **Unlimited:** $7/user/month (billed annually)
- **Business:** $12/user/month (billed annually)
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing
- **AI Add-on:** $9-$28/user/month extra

## Positioning
- "One app to replace them all" - all-in-one work platform
- Targets teams wanting to consolidate multiple tools
- Aggressive feature development, broad appeal

## Strengths
- Extremely feature-rich at low price point
- Free plan is generous (unlimited tasks)
- All-in-one approach reduces tool sprawl
- Strong customization options

## Weaknesses
- Feature bloat makes it overwhelming to learn
- Performance issues due to feature density
- AI features are expensive add-ons ($9-28/user extra)
- Inconsistent UX across different features

## Opportunities for TaskFlow
- Win on simplicity and focus vs ClickUp's "everything" approach
- Better performance through focused feature set
- AI included in pricing vs ClickUp's expensive add-ons
