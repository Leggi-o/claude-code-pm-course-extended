# Monday.com Competitor Analysis

## Features
- Visual project management (Kanban boards, Gantt charts, timeline views)
- Customizable workflows and automation
- Team collaboration and real-time updates
- Integration with 200+ apps (Slack, Salesforce, Google Workspace, etc.)
- Mobile app for iOS and Android
- Reporting and analytics dashboards
- Resource management and capacity planning
- Document management and file storage

## Pricing
- Free: Limited to 2 team members
- Basic: $9/user/month (annual billing)
- Standard: $12/user/month (annual billing)
- Pro: $19/user/month (annual billing)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

## Positioning
Target market: Mid-market to enterprise teams across marketing, IT, operations, and professional services. Positioned as a flexible, visual work management platform for complex team workflows and cross-functional collaboration.

## Strengths
- Highly visual and intuitive interface appeals to diverse user types
- Extensive marketplace of integrations and automations
- Strong brand recognition and market presence
- Scalable for growing organizations with enterprise features

## Weaknesses
- Per-user pricing becomes expensive for large teams
- Steep learning curve for advanced features
- Can feel bloated for small teams with simple workflows
- Limited offline functionality

## Opportunities for TaskFlow
- Target smaller, focused remote teams with simpler needs
- Offer better pricing for remote-first teams (flat-rate vs per-user)
- Build purpose-built collaboration features for distributed teams
